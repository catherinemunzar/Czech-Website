Příští Zastávka: Czech Republic

Next Stop: Czech Republic

Have you ever sat around, wondering how great of a country the Czech Republic is and even considered visiting it? Your answer is likely no. The presence of communism in Czech Republic has unfortunately made it developmentally behind and caused it to be seen as a poor, lesser-than country in Europe. Czech Republic has fought this stereotype that it has been given by boasting gorgeous castles, landscapes, and cities and being the home of a unique culture and history and delicious food.

Czech Republic is a Slavic, Eastern-European Country located at the heart of Europe. It’s prime location has caused it to be at the center of many historical conflicts, which has greatly influenced its culture. Home to the most Castles and the world's greatest beer, Czech Republic is a place you wouldn’t want to miss on your trip to Europe. This Website offers you a simple, yet in-depth peak into Czech history, culture, cuisine, art, and it even suggests locations to visit.
